Crispy Lumpia

Ingredients:

Lumpia Shanghai is a smaller, meatier type of egg roll that we Filipinos are very proud of! You could use pork, beef, or both. It's a very easy and yummy recipe that's great as finger food. We serve these spring rolls with sweet and sour sauce, soy sauce with lemon, or banana ketchup.

  • 50 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 3 cups cooking oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs ground pork
  • 2 pieces onion minced and carrots minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup parsley chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 medium egg

Directions:

  • Step 1. Combine all filling ingredients in a bowl. Mix well.
  • Step 2. Scoop around 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons of filling and place over a piece of lumpia wrapper.
  • Step 3. Spread the filling and then fold both sides of the wrapper. Fold the bottom.
  • Step 4. Brush beaten egg mixture on the top end of the wrapper.
  • Step 5. Roll-up until completely wrapped. Perform the same step until all mixture are consumed.
  • Step 6. eat oil in a cooking pot. Deep fry lumpia in medium heat until it floats.
